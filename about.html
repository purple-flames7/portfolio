Hi, I’m Katia.

I’m a web developer, creative thinker and lifelong learner. I started learning web development to open up more opportunities for myself and others and I’ve fallen in love with the power of turning ideas into interactive, inclusive digital experiences.

Why I Build Websites

For me, code is creative. I love that you can start with nothing, just an idea, and build something beautiful, functional and alive. Every project is a space to express myself, solve real problems, and design something that works for people.

What I Care About

I’m passionate about sustainability, inclusivity and community. I believe tech should serve everyone and I want to build with intention. I’m especially inspired by projects that align with the Sustainable Development Goals, amplify marginalized voices and expand access.

I bring a deep commitment to inclusivity and joy into every project, especially for communities that have historically been excluded. That perspective fuels my work.

A Little More About Me

I’m a quiet nerd with a big heart and a sharp sense of humor. I love good design, messy sketchbooks, great music, and big ideas. My friends joke that I can do anything, I take that as a challenge.
