Hi, I’m Katia.

I’m a web developer, creative thinker and lifelong learner. I started learning web development to open up new opportunities for myself and the communities I care about. With each project, I gain not just a new skill but a stronger sense of confidence in what I bring to the table. I’ve fallen in love with the power of turning ideas into interactive, inclusive digital experiences.

Why I Build Websites

I love how limitless it feels. The fact that you can start with nothing, just an idea, and build something beautiful, functional and alive. It's a space where logic meets imagination and I find deep joy in the process. Every project is a space to express myself, show up fully, solve real problems, and design something that matters and works for people.

What I Care About

I’m passionate about sustainability, inclusion and community. I believe tech should serve everyone and I want to build with intention. I’m especially inspired by projects that amplify marginalized voices and expand access.

I'm drawn to projects that challenge me, broaden my perspective and align with my values.

A Little More About Me

As a bit of a jack of all trades, people often joke that I can do anything and maybe they're not wrong. I'm a quiet nerd with a sharp sense of humor, a deep love for learning and a heart for impact.
